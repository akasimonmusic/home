About Sebastian Simon

As a musician and singer-songwriter, Sebastian Simon has crafted a style that is uniquely his own. He shares thoughtful reflections on life and his faith in Jesus Christ through a relaxed, positive vibey rhythm and music. His style is driven by a strong acoustic foundation, enhanced by creative, amplified sound elements that add a modern dynamic and perfectly complement his unique voice. With every song—from "Thank You, Lord" to "Gott gibt"—Sebastian invites the listener to rediscover faith and life in a fresh, authentic way.
